Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/28/2024
Effective date: 12/28/2024

At D'Guillermo Galeria, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment providers)
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and settings
  • Pages viewed and time spent on our website
  • Referring websites and search terms

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Process your purchases and deliver products
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)

Service Improvement

  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Improve our products and services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud

Data Security Measures

Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols during transmission.

Access Control

Strict access controls and authentication measures protect your data from unauthorized access.

Secure Storage

Data is stored in secure, monitored facilities with regular security audits and updates.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Team using the contact information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Third-Party Data Sharing
